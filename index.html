  • How to play?

You are to choose a category and answer the questions presented. The questions are based on statistics or numbers related to a certian event or phenomenon. The answers are not exact because the point is not to get the right exact value rather grasp the scale of the event/phenomenon in question. Move the slider across the range given to select the answer. The answer does not have to be exact but it can also be the nearest value (+ or -) to the correct answer.

  • Scoring

Think of a dartboard, the closer to the center the higher the score!
For each correct answer that is nearest neighbor to the correct answer, you get 1 point (a free throw in basketball!). As your answer becomes exact, you get 3 points (a three pointer!). If you choose a wrong answer, the game ends!
