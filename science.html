  • How to play?

You are to choose a category and answer the questions presented. The questions are based on statistics or numbers related to a certian event or phenomenon. Move the slider across the range given to select the answer. The answer does not have to be exact but it has to be within 20% to the true correct answer.

  • Difficulty setting

After getting 10 correct answers, the difficulty level goes up, so now your answer has to be within the range of 10% of the correct answer. After another 10 correct answers, you have to guess the exact correct answer.

  • Scoring

Think of a dartboard, the closer to the center the higher the score!
For each correct answer 20% far from the true answer, you get 1 point (a free throw in basketball!). Within 10% of the true answer, you get 2 points. As your answer becomes exact, you get 3 points (a three pointer!!). If you choose a wrong answer, the game ends!
