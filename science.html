  • How to play?

You are to choose a category and answer the questions presented. The questions are based on statistics or numbers related to a certian event or phenomenon. Move the slider across the range given to select the answer. The answer does not have to be exact but it can also be the nearest value (+ or -) to the correct answer.

  • Difficulty setting

After getting 10 correct answers, the difficulty level goes up, so now your answer has to be the exact correct answer.

  • Scoring

Think of a dartboard, the closer to the center the higher the score!
For each correct answer that is nearest neighbor to the correct answer, you get 1 point (a free throw in basketball!). As your answer becomes exact, you get 3 points (a three pointer!!). If you choose a wrong answer, the game ends!
